1	food is put into mouth.
2	food is chewed and mixed with saliva (spit).
3	food travels down the esophagus (throat).
4	the food travels into the stomach.
5	acid in the stomach breaks the food down further.
6	food travels into the small intestine.
7	nutrients from food are absorbed into the blood stream.
8	food travels into the large intestine.
9	most of the remaining material is now the waste.
10	the waste is eventually removed from the body.
