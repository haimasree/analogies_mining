1	Aluminium cans are put into a recycling bin.
2	Aluminium cans are then collected and taken to a treatment plant.
3	At the treatment plant the aluminium is sorted and cleaned.
4	Readied for reprocessing.
5	The aluminum cans go through a re-melt process.
6	Turned into molten aluminium.
7	The molten aluminum is made into large blocks called ingots.
8	The ingots are sent to mills and are rolled out into thin sheets.
9	The rolls are ready to be made into new aluminum products.
